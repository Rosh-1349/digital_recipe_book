Chow Mein

⭐ Rating: 0.0

Prep Time: 15 mins
Cook Time: 15 mins
Serves: 3-4

Start Cooking

Ingredients

  • 200g noodles
  • 1 cup cabbage, shredded
  • 1/2 cup carrots, julienned
  • 1/2 cup bell peppers, sliced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp chili sauce
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 2 tsp oil
  • 1 tsp garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil noodles as per package instructions. Drain and toss with a little oil.
  2. Heat oil in a wok. Add garlic and sauté for a few seconds.
  3. Add vegetables and stir-fry on high heat for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add soy sauce, chili sauce, vinegar, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  5. Add noodles and toss everything together on high flame for 2 more minutes.
  6. Serve hot with ketchup or schezwan sauce.
