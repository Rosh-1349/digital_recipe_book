Pav Bhaji

⭐ Rating: 0.0

Prep Time: 15 mins
Cook Time: 30 mins
Serves: 4

Start Cooking

Ingredients

  • 4 pav buns
  • 2 potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1/2 cup green peas
  • 1/2 cup cauliflower, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup chopped capsicum
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1-2 tsp pav bhaji masala
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Lemon wedges & chopped coriander (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Heat butter, sauté onions and ginger-garlic paste till golden.
  2. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  3. Add all vegetables, spices, and a little water. Cook and mash until well-blended.
  4. Simmer the bhaji for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Toast pav buns with butter on a tawa until golden brown.
  6. Serve hot bhaji with toasted pav, lemon, and chopped coriander.
